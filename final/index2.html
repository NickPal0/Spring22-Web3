What we are

Ur Mix is a program that helps chefs from all age ranges and background find their footing in the culinary industry. Taking the first step into gettign inolved can be a dauting task. So we are here to help those who wish to pursue a career in cooking. We do so by setting up individuals with either culuinary schools, or jobs that we find suit them the best based on what they wish to do and where they want to go on their culiary journey. From working in world class resturants around the world, to working as a professor is the top culinary schools in the country. We are here to provide that opporunity to those who are willing to put in the work. There have been individuals who have gone off and started their own sucessful buisnesses by taking what they learned from resturant jobs, we aided them in finding, and building off of that. It is truly incredible experience to see people chance their dreams and succeed. Which is why we love to help in anyway we can to those who need it.
