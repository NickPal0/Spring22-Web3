The store

Named after the protected, El Segundo, Butterfly, Blue Butterfly Coffee Co. has been locally owned and operated for 26 years. For 26 years we have shared coffee, food we love, and our lives with our community. Proprietors Dale & Toni Inghram, are active members in the El Segundo community. Dale Inghram is a certified coffee Q-Grader. He founded a second coffee roasting company, Smoky Hollow Coffee Roasters. Blue Butterfly Coffee Co. sources all of their coffee beans from Smoky Hollow Coffee Roasters.
